# RFM Segment Guide

## What is RFM?

**RFM** stands for:
- **R (Recency):** How recently did customer last purchase?
- **F (Frequency):** How often do they purchase?
- **M (Monetary):** How much do they spend?

Each metric is scored 1-5 (5 being best), creating 8 distinct customer segments.

## 8 Customer Segments

### ⭐ Champions
- **Best customers** - Recent, frequent, high spenders
- **Count:** 16,274 (16%)
- **Revenue:** \.26M (42% of total)
- **Action:** VIP treatment, exclusive benefits
- **Example:** Buys every month, spent \+

### 💚 Loyal Customers
- **Consistent buyers** - Regular purchases
- **Count:** 7,731 (8%)
- **Revenue:** \ (7%)
- **Action:** Loyalty rewards, early access
- **Example:** Buys every 2-3 months, spent \+

### 🚀 Potential Loyalists
- **Growth opportunity** - Frequent but can spend more
- **Count:** 7,836 (8%)
- **Revenue:** \.91M (19%)
- **Action:** Cross-sell, personalize
- **Example:** Buys 4-5 times, high value per order

### ⚠️ At Risk
- **Losing interest** - Recent but infrequent
- **Count:** 15,277 (15%)
- **Revenue:** \ (6%)
- **Action:** Win-back offers, re-engage
- **Example:** Last bought 2 months ago, only 1-2 purchases total

### 🚨 Can't Lose Them
- **URGENT** - Was valuable, now dormant
- **Count:** 11,805 (12%)
- **Revenue:** \ (6%)
- **Action:** Urgent outreach, special comeback offer
- **Example:** Used to buy regularly, inactive 6+ months

### 📊 Need Attention
- **Medium value** - Moderate engagement
- **Count:** 15,889 (16%)
- **Revenue:** \.65M (26%)
- **Action:** Targeted campaigns, recommendations
- **Example:** Buys occasionally, medium spender

### 💀 Lost
- **Very inactive** - Long time since purchase
- **Count:** 19,642 (20%)
- **Revenue:** \.69M (27%)
- **Action:** Final offer or remove from list
- **Example:** Last purchase 1+ year ago

### 📋 Others
- **Unclassified** - New or edge cases
- **Count:** 3,753 (4%)
- **Revenue:** \ (1%)
- **Action:** General marketing

## Quick Decision Tree

\\\
Is recency recent (< 3 months)?
├─ YES
│  ├─ Frequent (4+ purchases)?
│  │  ├─ YES → High spender? → YES = CHAMPIONS | NO = POTENTIAL
│  │  └─ NO → AT RISK
│  └─ Not frequent (1-2 purchases)?
│     └─ NEED ATTENTION
└─ NO (3+ months ago)
   ├─ Was frequent? → CAN'T LOSE THEM
   └─ Wasn't frequent? → LOST
\\\
